Welcome to:

Design-Build-Educate Inc.

Guidelines for Proposed Project:

  • involves such a variety of skills and interests so as to require a fairly large group of students for its completion.
  • can challenge the brightest students yet be within the capabilities of the less talented students.
  • can be completed outside the structured school environment(e.g. at home).
  • has adult support(consultants with specialized talents as well as school employees who have connections with the students that consultants do not).

Project Goal:

Give youth opportunities to gain
experience in the following areas:

  1. Business Management
  2. Electrical Engineering
  3. Electronic Technology
  4. Computer Science
  5. Photography/Videography
  6. Education

Project: Objectives

  1. Design and build an
    8-bit computer
  2. Test and Train
  3. Film all activities
  4. Build a company Website
